Penguins

Penguins can be found in the iciest part of Anika's Aquatic Animals!

Penguins are flightless seabirds that can be found living below the equator. Half their lives are spent on land, and the other half in sea. Here are some fun facts:

☆ A group of penguins is called a raft in water but a waddle on land.

☆ Penguins have evolved to fly underwater.

☆ In order to charm female penguins, male penguins gift the females with rocks.
