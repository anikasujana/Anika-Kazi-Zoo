Sea Lions

Sea lions can be found in the tropical waters of Anika's Aquatic Animals!

Sea lions are characterized by external ear flaps, the ability to walk on all fours, long foreflippers, big chest and belly, and short and thick hair. Here are some fun facts:

☆ Sea lions produce loud roars, which is why they're named after lions.

☆ Most sea lion pups are born in late June.

☆ Sea lions are social animals and can be often seen in large groups, both on sea and land.
