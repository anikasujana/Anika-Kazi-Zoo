Pandas

The giant panda, sometimes called a panda bear or simply panda, is a bear species endemic to China.

Like media shows, pandas love bamboo. Pandas actually need at least 2 different bamboo species in their range to avoid starvation. I wonder what bamboo tastes like. Here are some fun facts:

☆ Although the pandas seem easy to spot with their white and black fur, it is actually used for camoflauge and communication!

☆ Mother pandas make sure that their cub is with them 100% of the time during the cub's first month!

☆ Pandas are excellent swimmers and learn to climb trees around three months old! They typically practice by climbing their mother's back.
