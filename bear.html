Bears

Bears are known to inhabit both coniferous and deciduous forests. They also live in alpine habitats and along river courses.

Bears are one of the most dangerous animals in the world! But, why friend shaped if not friend? Here are some fun facts:

☆ Bears can stand anywhere between 3 to 9 feet tall!

☆ Although bears have quite average sight and hearing, they're sense of smell makes them great predators!

☆ Cubs are born blind and stay in their den for up to 3 months!
