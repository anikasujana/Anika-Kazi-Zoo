Turtles

Turtles can be found in both the sea and land of Anika's Aquatic Animals!

Turtle shells are made mainly of bone, and they are ectotherms, or cold-blooded animals. Here are some fun facts:

☆ The sex of sea turtles are determined by the temperature of the sand that their nest is in.

☆ Leatherback sea turtles have existed in their current form since the age of the dinosaurs.

☆ Sea turtles don't retract into their shells.
