Owls

Owls live in many different habitats, including coniferous forests, mountains, deserts, and plains.

Owls are some of the deadliest predators in the world! Stuart Little better watch out! Here are some fun facts:

☆ Owls can rotate their necks 270 degrees!

☆ Many owls have assymetrical ears to help them listen and find their prey

☆ A group of owls is called a parliament. This actually comes from the famous book series, The Chronicles of Narnia by C.S. Lewis!
