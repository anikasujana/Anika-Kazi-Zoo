Giraffes

Giraffes are mainly found in savannas and open woodlands!

Giraffes are the tallest mammals in the world by being able to grow up to 18 feet! That has to be like at least 50% of Hunter College. Here are some fun facts:

☆ Giraffes usually only sleep a few minutes at a time. Helping them protect themselves against predators!

☆ Although giraffes are so tall, their markings help them camoflauge in trees!

☆ The giraffe that is most popular in modern media is the Masai Giraffe. It's star-like pattern is what makes it different from other species of giraffes!
