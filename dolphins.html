Dolphins

Sea lions can found in the colder waters of Anika's Aquatic Animals!

Dolphins have streamlined bodies and two limbs that are used as flippers. They are fast animals, and have hearing that is adapted for both air and water. Here are some fun facts:

☆ Dolphins only sleep with half their brain.

☆ Some whales are actually categorized as dolphins.

☆ The size of a dolphin can often be determined by their sex; male dolphins are larger than female dolphins.
